Help Page

Welcome to Vaccine Management System, a website aimed to increase the ease of following a proper vaccination plan. We hope to help our users as much as possible. Here is a few guidelines if you face any issue:

Registration Requirements:

  • Username: For username, it is required to use 150 characters or fewer. Letters, digits and @/./+/-/_ only. Any other characters will be considered invalid.
  • Password: For password ensure to follow the given rules
    • Your password cannot be too similar to your other personal information.
    • Your password must contain at least 8 characters.
    • Your password cannot be a commonly used password.
    • Your password cannot be entirely numeric.
    • After entering username and password, you are required to confirm your password.
    • If the password is valid, then your account is created. You can log in to the website after registration
    • If username and password is invalid, there will be guidelines to solve the problem.

Profile Requirements:

  • You are required to update your profile information. The information must be valid.
    • Fill out your name as per your national ID, passport or any official identification.
    • Enter your age
    • Enter proper contact information like your phone number and email id, so that we can easily communicate with you
    • Enter your permanent address
  • You can view or edit your profile at any time you want

Schedule Appointments:

  • You can schedule appointments any time you want
    • Fill out city name
    • Choose any of the available clinics as per your choice
    • Choose between the dates
    • Choose between the available timeslot. You can pick between morning times or evening times.
  • If the scheduling is successful, you will receive a confirmation mail.
  • You can Read/Check appointments at any time you want

Reschedule Appointments:

  • You can reschedule appointments any time you want
    • Fill out city name
    • Choose any of the available clinics as per your choice
    • Choose between the dates
    • Choose between the available timeslot. You can pick between morning times or evening times.
  • If the rescheduling is successful, you will receive a confirmation mail.
  • You can Read/Check appointments at any time you want

In order to cancel appointments, go to cancel appointments can delete the appointment.
